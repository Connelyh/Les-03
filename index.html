Healthy pasta Shizzle

What do you need?

  • Pasta
  • 75g Cashewnuts
  • a big handful of basil
  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Lemon
  • 2 cloves of roasted garlic
  • 5 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
